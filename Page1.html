Pagination

What is Pagination?

Pagination is a UI/UX design technique that divides large amounts of content into smaller, manageable pages. It helps users navigate through data or content in an organized way, instead of scrolling endlessly.

Key Features of Pagination:

  • Divides content into multiple pages.
  • Improves user experience by making navigation easier.
  • Commonly used in blogs, search results, and data tables.
  • Typically includes "Previous" and "Next" buttons along with numbered pages.
